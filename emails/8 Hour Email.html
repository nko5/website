8 Hour Email

Node Knockout starts in just 8 hours. PLEASE be sure to check off the items below before the competition begins. We do not want any issues to arise during the competition and distract you from creating your apps. 

We are around to answer all questions immediately via email.

- View your team page and make sure all your members have accepted their invites.

- Make sure you have access to your team Github repository <GITHUB_URL>. Check that all your team members have joined your GitHub team.

- Make sure you have access to your Modulus hosting account. They sent a “Forgot Password” email to each user in order to set a password. If you don't have access, or didn't receive that, please email us right away.

- Follow the instructions on the README.md of your Github repo and DEPLOY THE SAMPLE APP NOW. This is just to make sure everything is functional and so you won't run into any nasty hosting issues during the 48hrs. <GITHUB_URL>

- PLEASE DONT CHECK ANYTHING INTO THE REPO BEFORE THE COMPETITION OFFICIALLY STARTS. IF YOU DO, IT MAY DISQUALIFY YOUR TEAM.

- Need redis? - Sign up for a free RedisGreen Mini Development server with a monitoring dashboard for use during the competition, visit: https://dashboard.redisgreen.net/nk_team/sign_up

- Need PostgreSQL, RethinkDB, ElasticSearch, RabbitMQ, or etcd? Email us and we'll set you up. Or if there's another paid service or API you'd like to use, let us know and we'll try to get you an account!

- Make sure to join the Node Knockout IRC channel #nodeknockout (on freenode). We will try to have support staff around during the entire competition so if you run into issues let us know there as well.

Don't forget to use our service sponsors! They will help you be more productive over the weekend. There are only 48 hours so take any help you can get!

Also, npm is now sponsoring! They're offering their hosted package manager to teams, in addition to some prizes. You can sign up https://www.npmjs.com/signup
